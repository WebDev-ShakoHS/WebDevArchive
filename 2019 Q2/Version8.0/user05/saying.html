Top 5 Most common Vsco Sayings

RankSaying

2And I oop
3Save the Turtles
4Spill the Tea
5Hydroflask

SKSKSKSK

SKSKSK is similar to a laugh. Usually is said after something funny or when they drop their hydroflasks.

And I oop

And I oop is a saying meaning you're caught off guard or something unexpected has happened.

Save the turtles

A common staple of the VSCO culture is being very enviornmentally friendly and striving for a healthier earth. This is a common saying as a way to combat plastic pollution in the oceans.

Spill the tea

Not actual tea, it means to share the gossip or secret.

Hydroflask

A form of water bottle commonly paired with the VSCO culture. It is reusable so there is minimal waste produced.
