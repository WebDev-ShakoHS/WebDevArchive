Pancake description

Pancakes are light and fluffy cooked disks of batter that are usually cooked on a stovetop or griddle. After cooked and are ready to be eaten, they can be paired with multiple toppings such as syrup, chocolate chips, whipped cream, and sometimes sprinkles along with other assortments of fruits (if you're that kind of person).
