Hi! I'm Michelle, a high school senior and pianist of 11 years. But in spite of my experience, I've always struggled because of my small hands. I've had to modify countless chords and passages to fit my fingers. As a result, I've made this website to help out my fellow small-handed pianists. Don't let our literal shortcomings hinder your passion for music!
