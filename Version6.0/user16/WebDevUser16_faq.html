How long have you played piano?
11 years. I started in the summer of 2008.
How do I practice more?
I'm sure you've been told to log your practice hours or set aside a specific time slot to practice, and while those practices do help, it really comes down to personal enjoyment. Pick songs you WANT to learn.
How long should I practice?
It depends. For beginners, I recommend anywhere from 25 to 30 minutes. As for more advanced students, I'd suggest 1 to 2 hours (with breaks).
What should I practice?
Scales. For the love of God, Blease practice your scales. If you are unwilling to practice anything else, practice your scales.
What is the hardest part of playing piano?
The hardest part of piano, I believe, is interpretation. Different musical eras require nuances that can make or break a song. And the worst part? It's not something you can learn directly from a book.
